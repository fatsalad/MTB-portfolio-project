About Madame The Band

Raw and uncut, Madame The Band is a one woman production featuring singer-song writer and multi-instrumentalist Cassondra Cravens. Through raging vocals and pounding drums, MTB delivers an eclectic mix of blues and soul inspired indie works that slip and slide from upbeat dance jams to heart wrenching ballads. Having toured the coasts of California and having performed in venues such as The Glasshouse and The House of Blues, Cravens brings a seasoned punch of intensity to every single show that leaves a lasting impression on all who witness.
